## Alpha Skill - RED Dark Matter

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HydrosPlays/ultrarumbleguide/refs/heads/main/images/redo1.png" /></br>
</p>

Fires a spinning **RED** mist. Remains in place for a set time after impact or at max range.

## Beta Skill - RED Warp Gate 

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HydrosPlays/ultrarumbleguide/refs/heads/main/images/redo2.png" /></br>
</p>

Short-press to link two distant spaces with mist. Hold to send mist and release to set the distance. If the mist hits an opponent while you send it out, it teleports them in front of you.

## Gamma Skill - RED Shadow Haunt

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HydrosPlays/ultrarumbleguide/refs/heads/main/images/redo3.png" /></br>
</p>

Creates **RED** mist that lets you see and move from its perspective. Jump to rise, crouch to descend. If the mist is hit, it disappears and you return to normal view.

## Gamma Skill (Input a Quirk Skill) - RED Shadow Haunt Raid

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HydrosPlays/ultrarumbleguide/refs/heads/main/images/redo3.png" /></br>
</p>

When in use, inputting Quirk Skill α or β will fire from the mist instead. Short-press Quirk Skill γ to warp to the mist, and hold to return to your original location.

## Special Action - RED Bail Out

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HydrosPlays/ultrarumbleguide/refs/heads/main/images/redo4.png" /></br>
</p>

Warps a downed ally to your location. Works no matter where they are.
